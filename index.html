How to Play Quantum Tic-Tac-Toe

Quantum Tic-Tac-Toe introduces quantum mechanics to the classic game. Each turn, players place two entangled marks. When cycles form, measurements occur and collapses happen. The first player to get three classical marks in a row wins.
